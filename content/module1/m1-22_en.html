Emergencies Act

Enables the federal government to fulfill its constitutional responsibility to provide for the safety and security of Canadians during national emergencies that are considered extraordinary and are not covered in other federal legislation.

Emergency Management Act

The legislative foundation that assigns specific responsibilities to federal ministers to ensure an integrated approach to federal emergency management activities.

Federal Policy for Emergency Management

Promotes an integrated and resilient whole-of-government approach to emergency management planning, which includes better prevention/mitigation of, preparedness for, response to, and recovery from emergencies.

Emergency Management Framework for Canada

A common framework for federal/provincial/territorial emergency management initiatives that aims to help ensure more coherent, complementary actions among the different FPT governmental initiatives.
