Checkpoint: Changing Threats

As we have seen, emergency management is becoming increasingly important for all jurisdictions in Canada, including the federal government.

The following factors are contributing to our changing threat environment.

  • environment and climate change
  • increased urbanization
  • animal and human diseases
  • dependencies and interdependencies on official infrastructure

These are just some of the reasons for the increase in number and frequency of natural and human-induced emergencies in Canada and around the world.
