Recap – Lesson 1: Emergencies in Canada

Let’s pause for a quick recap of the key points to remember about emergencies in Canada.

Select each heading for a summary of lesson 1.

Definition of Emergency

A present or imminent event or incident that requires prompt coordination of actions concerning persons or property to protect the health, safety or welfare of people, or to limit damage to property or the environment.

Types of Emergencies

Natural and human-induced

Changing Threats to Canada

Increased threats due to:

  • environment and climate change
  • increased urbanization
  • critical infrastructure dependencies and interdependencies
  • terrorism
  • animal and human diseases
  • increased movement of people and goods around the world
