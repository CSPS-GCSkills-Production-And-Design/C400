Emergency Management Organizations (continued)

Emergency management organizations play a key role in provincial/territorial emergency management programs in many ways. EMO's:

  • provide for the development of effective emergency management programs within their jurisdiction
  • develop and coordinate overall plans and programs so the provincial/territorial government is prepared for emergencies
  • work with various federal government organizations, provincial/territorial government departments, and service organizations to develop and maintain a wide range of emergency management programs
  • help municipalities, industries and aboriginal communities develop their plans and emergency management programs
  • coordinate the provincial response activities during a major emergency
  • promote and support standards to support the training and exercise of emergency plans
  • provide recovery programs best suited to the needs of the individual jurisdiction.
