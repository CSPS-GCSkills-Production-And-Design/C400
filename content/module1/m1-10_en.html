Definition and Purpose

As you will see throughout this course, arriving at a common understanding of terms and objectives is key to effective cooperation among emergency management professionals.

Click on the two headings for the definition and purpose of emergency management.

Definition of Emergency Management Act

The Emergency Management Act defines emergency management as:

  • prevention and mitigation of emergencies
  • preparedness for emergencies
  • response to emergencies
  • recovery from emergencies.
Purpose of emergency management in Canada

The purpose of emergency management in Canada is to:

  • save lives
  • preserve the environment
  • protect property
  • protect the economy.
