Exercising the Plan

No plan is complete until it has been exercised. The goal of an EM exercise is to improve operational readiness.

In emergency management, an exercise is a simulated scenario in which one or more organizations practices their respective response activities to test emergency plans.

Exercises are designed to test and validate plans, policies, procedures, training, equipment and inter-agency agreements during a simulated event to see how the participants would perform in an actual emergency.

Exercising a plan typically follows five phases.

Did You Know?

Essentially, there are 2 different types of exercises which test emergency management plans.

Discussion-based exercises:

  • seminars
  • workshops
  • tabletop exercises
  • immersive simulations (or gaming)

Operations-based exercises:

  • drills
  • functional exercises
  • full scale exercises
