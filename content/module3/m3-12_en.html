Types of Emergency Management Plans

Emergency plans guide the actions of emergency management activities and are an integral part of the preparedness component. Some jurisdictions prefer to plan for all hazards and others develop their plans based on specific hazards determined from their risk assessments.

During this next section, we will concentrate on just a few important government plans which all federal organizations need to be aware of.

  • Federal Emergency Response Plan (FERP)
  • Emergency Management Plan (EMP)
  • Operational Plans
  • Business Continuity Plans (BCP)
  • Canada's Continuity of Constitutional Government Emergency Response and Recovery Plan (CERRP)
