Training

Training means much more than merely teaching skills or instructing someone in certain concepts or procedures. To enable an organization to function as an effective whole, training also includes:

  • integrating skills to build organizational capacity
  • practising skills
  • implementing various procedures to help ensure a smoothly coordinated operation
