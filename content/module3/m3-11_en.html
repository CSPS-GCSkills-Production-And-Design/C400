Did You Know?

Most freight trains carry dangerous materials. In addition, one truck in ten carries hazardous items on major highways through communities.

Greater perception and awareness of hazards and threats

We now have a greater awareness of the hazards and threats we face. There has been an increasing number of terrorist attacks on western world countries, increasing public health concerns, and improved capability to forecast severe weather events.

Increasing frequency of naturally-occuring emergencies

As a result of rapid climate change, the number of natural emergencies is increasing and will continue to do so. For example, we continue to see a significant rise in the number of severe weather events in recent years.

Increasing dependence on technology

An increased dependence on modern technology and electricity makes us more vulnerable to disruptions, many of which can be disastrous.

Increase in transportation of dangerous goods

There has been an increase in the transportation of dangerous goods across the globe and within Canada.
