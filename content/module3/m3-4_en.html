What is Preparedness?

What is emergency preparedness?

Preparedness is one of the four components of emergency management.

Click on “Definition” for the meaning of preparedness in the context of emergency management.

Definition

Preparedness

It is defined in the Emergency Management Framework for Canada as: “To be ready to respond to a disaster and manage its consequences through measures taken prior to an event, for example emergency response plans, mutual assistance agreements, resource inventories and training, equipment and exercise programs.”
