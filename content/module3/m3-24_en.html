Continuous Improvement of Federal Event Response

The Continuous Improvement of Federal Event Response (CIFER) program seeks to identify and address gaps in federal all-hazards event response capabilities to support enhanced readiness of the federal government and provide evidence to support decision-making by senior officials.

It does so by gathering, analyzing, prioritizing, tracking and reporting on recommendations or improvement actions relevant to all or part of the federal all-hazards event response community. The recommendations originate with after-action reports and after-incident reports written for events or exercises involving multiple federal organizations.
