Emergency Management Act

The legislative foundation providing for a coordinated approach to federal EM activities. The Minister of PS is responsible for exercising emergency management leadership and advising other federal Ministers on their legislative responsibilities as they relate to emergency management planning. The EMA sets out clear roles and responsibilities for all federal ministers for emergencies in Canada.

Federal Policy for Emergency Management

Directs federal organizations to:

  • have a sustainable capacity to meet the goals outlined in their respective emergency management plans
  • conduct or participate in exercises to test and implement their emergency management plans, and participate in training
  • incorporate lessons learned and best practices in the emergency management planning process
  • include measures to provide for the continuity of operation in their emergency management plans
  • provide the Government Operations Centre with information related to national-level situational awareness
  • share post-exercise and post-event information with Public Safety Canada.
Securing on Open Society: National Security Policy

Help ensure that Canada is prepared for and can respond to current and future security threats.

Policy on Government Security

Identifies lead security agencies and organisations that have a leadership and support role and that contribute to the achievement of government security policy objectives.
