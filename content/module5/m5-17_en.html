Recap – Lesson 2: Planning for Recovery

Here is a recap of the strategies, plans and steps involved in emergency recovery.

Click on each heading for a recap of lesson two.

Strategies

A recovery strategy needs to support short- and long-term priorities for recovery of functions, services, resources, facilities and infrastructure. It provides the basis for which the recovery plans are based.

Plans

The objective is to provide for the restoration and continuity of critical services and operations.

This should be built into each federal organization’s Emergency Management Plan.

Four Steps of Recovery

  1. Undertake systemic restoration.
  2. Demobilize resources.
  3. Resume normal operations.
  4. Conduct post-event evaluation.
