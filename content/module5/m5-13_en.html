4 Steps of Recovery: Step 1

There are four steps involved in planning for the recovery phase of an emergency. Each step involves a number of activities.

Step 1: Undertake systemic restoration

  • Conduct a damage assessment
  • Estimate the cost to repair or replace the damage
  • Determine courses of action for a pre-emergency condition
  • Conduct a cost benefit analysis
  • Select the most viable and cost-efficient options
  • Initiate planning for restoration of activities
