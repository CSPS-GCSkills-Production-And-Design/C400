Recap – Lesson 1: The Driving Force for Recovery

Here is a quick recap of the legislative and policy driving force for the Government of Canada's responsibilities for assisting in emergency recovery.

Click on each heading for a recap of lesson one.

Emergency Management Act
  • Sets out clear roles and responsibilities for all federal ministers across the full spectrum of emergency management, including recovery, for emergencies in Canada.
  • Assigns responsibility to the Minister of Public Safety for participating, in accordance with Canada’s foreign relations policies, in international EM activities.

Federal Policy for Emergency Management

Directs federal organizations to:

  • include provisions for recovery assistance to provincial/territorial governments in their departmental EM plans
  • undertake post-recovery analysis and incorporate lessons learned and best practices into their emergency management plans
