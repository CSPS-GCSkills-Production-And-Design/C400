Some Questions for You

Before we begin, here are a couple questions for you; let’s see what you already know about the Government of Canada’s role in emergency recovery.

Think about the 2019 spring flooding in Ontario, Quebec, and New Brunswick, or the 2016 Wildfire in Fort McMurray, Alberta.

What kind of involvement do you think the federal government had in the recovery processes for both?

Can you name any Government of Canada activities that supported the recovery efforts?

Take the time to think of an answer, then note your response on a piece of paper.

We will come back to your response later in this module.
