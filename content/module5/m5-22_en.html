Federal Disaster Assistance Programs

The federal government provides over 20 recovery assistance programs from various departments and agencies.

To…

  • individuals and households
  • farms and agriculture
  • local authorities and community groups
  • businesses

For….

  • financial support
  • facilities and supplies
  • information resources

The Disaster Assistance Programs - List of all federal programs page, on the Public Safety Canada website, provides a listing of recovery assistance programs available in the event of disasters, or emergencies affecting Canadian citizens, businesses and communities.

Did You Know?

One of many federal disaster assistance programs includes the Compensation Program for Destroyed Animals.

The Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA) awards compensation to the owners of animals ordered destroyed by the federal government in order to control the spread of animal disease.
