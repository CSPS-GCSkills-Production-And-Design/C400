Disaster Financial Assistance Arrangements (DFAA)

When response and recovery costs exceed what individual provinces or territories could reasonably be expected to bear on their own, the DFAA provides the Government of Canada with a fair and equitable means of assisting the provincial and territorial governments.

Federal Disaster Assistance Programs

The federal government provides over 20 recovery assistance programs from various departments and agencies:

To:

  • individuals and households
  • farms and agriculture
  • local authorities and community groups
  • businesses

For:

  • financial support
  • facilities and supplies
  • information resources
