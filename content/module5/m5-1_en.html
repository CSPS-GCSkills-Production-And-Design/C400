Learning Objectives

Completing this module will enable you to:

  • define recovery in the context of emergency management
  • identify the driving force (i.e. the legislation and policies) behind the Government of Canada’s responsibilities for emergency recovery
  • explain why it is important to develop and implement a recovery strategy
  • state the objectives of a recovery plan
  • list the four steps involved in the Government of Canada’s recovery efforts
  • describe the Disaster Financial Assistance Arrangements Program
