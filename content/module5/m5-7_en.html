EM in Action: Canada

In Canada, most of the large-scale disasters are weather-related events.

Climate change is having an impact by increasing the frequency of flooding, and making wildfires in Canada bigger, hotter, and more dangerous.

In the event of a large-scale natural disaster, the Government of Canada may provide financial assistance to provincial and territorial governments when the response and recovery costs place a significant burden on and would exceed what individual provinces and territories might reasonably be expected to bear on their own.
