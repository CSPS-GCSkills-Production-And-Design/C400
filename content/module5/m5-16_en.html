4 Steps of Recovery: Step 4

Step 4: Conduct post-event evaluation

  • Identify any materials and logistics stores that need to be replenished.
  • Provide a copy of the post-evaluation report to the Government Operations Centre.
  • Meet with first responders, support staff, the incident management team, communications staff and management to:
    • compare notes
    • identify lessons learned
    • finalize evaluation report
    • propose changes to plans and training
    • propose mitigation options
    • propose scenarios for future exercises
    • propose improvements in communication and reporting
