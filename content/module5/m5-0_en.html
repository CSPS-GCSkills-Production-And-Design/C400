Introduction

Welcome to the Recovery module, the last pillar of emergency management.

Recovery begins after the immediate threats to people and infrastructure have subsided. Recovering from an emergency involves more than just replacing what has been destroyed or rehabilitating those directly affected. Recovery is also a complex social process.

Preparing for the recovery process begins before any emergency occurs with the inclusion of recovery programs and activities being built into an emergency management plan.
