Some Questions for You

This phase of the emergency management process focuses on preventing and mitigating emergencies in the future, and often looks to the past for lessons learned.

Before we begin, here is a question for you, to see what you already know about the Government of Canada’s emergency prevention/mitigation measures.

Think about a past emergency that threatened the lives, property or environment of Canadian citizens.

What measures are now being taken to avoid or minimize the impact if a similar type of emergency were to take place again? Who is responsible for those prevention/mitigation efforts?

Take the time to think of an answer, then note your response on a piece of paper. We will come back to your response later in this module.
