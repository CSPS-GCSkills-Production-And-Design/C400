Learning Objectives

This prevention and mitigation module will enable you to:

  • Define prevention and mitigation in the context of emergency management.
  • Identify the driving force (i.e. the legislation and policy) behind the Government of Canada’s emergency prevention/mitigation activities.
  • Define important risk assessment terminology.
  • Describe the four steps of risk assessment.
  • Differentiate between structural and non-structural measures to reduce the impact of hazards.
  • Name the key federal strategies and measures that strengthen Canada’s resiliency and help prevent or mitigate the hazards and risks of emergencies.
