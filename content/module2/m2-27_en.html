Prevention and Mitigation Actions and Measures

Actions taken to eliminate or reduce the impacts and risks of hazards through structural or non-structural measures taken before an emergency or disaster occurs.

Disaster Mitigation and Adaptation Fund

$2 billion investment in large-scale infrastructure projects to help communities better manage risks of natural disasters.

National Strategy for Critical Infrastructure

Aims to enhance the resiliency of critical infrastructure against current and emerging hazards. It supports a strong collaborative approach between federal, provincial and territorial governments and the 10 critical infrastructure sectors.
