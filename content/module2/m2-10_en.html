  1. Identify the risks and capabilities (by identifying and describing hazards and threats)
  1. Analyze the risks
    • evaluate the likelihood/probability of the occurrence
    • analyze the consequences/impact
    • consider vulnerabilities
  1. Evaluate the risks
    • prioritize the risks
  1. Identify the risk prevention/mitigation options
    • avoid the risk
    • remove the source of the risk
    • change the nature or magnitude of the likelihood
    • change the consequences or impact
    • share the risk with another party
    • retain or ignore the risk

Did You Know?

The Integrated Terrorism Assessment Centre (ITAC) went into operation in October 2004 as a result of the National Security Policy’s measures to address terrorist threats.

ITAC is the only federal organization specifically tasked to analyze terrorism threats to Canada and Canadian interest worldwide, based on security intelligence from partner agencies in Canada and abroad. ITAC is the only source of community-wide terrorism assessments for the Government of Canada.
