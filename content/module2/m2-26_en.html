EM in Action: Wingham (continued)

One thousand homeowners in Wingham received a rain barrel with a capacity to hold 203 litres (45 gallons) of water. A public education campaign and information sessions generated support for the program.

Wingham residents were reminded that this simple preventive measure would help them:

  • reduce/eliminate the risk of their house or property being flooded
  • save money on their water bill
  • reduce demand for township-treated water
  • do their part to help reduce the number of sewer overload incidences in the Wingham community
  • help the environment and set an example for other communities.
