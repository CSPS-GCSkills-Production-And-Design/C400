What is emergency prevention/mitigation?

“Actions taken to adapt to, eliminate or reduce the risk and/or impact of disasters in order to protect lives, property, and the environment, and reduce economic disruption.  Prevention/mitigation includes structural mitigative measures (e.g. construction of floodways and dykes) and non-structural mitigative measures (e.g. buidling codes, land-use planning, and insurance incentives).  Prevention and mitigation may be considered independently or one may include the other.”


Emergency Management Framework for Canada (2017)
