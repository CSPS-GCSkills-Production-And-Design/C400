Geological Hazards

  • Earthquake
  • Tsunami
  • Volcano
  • Landslide
  • Glacier, iceberg


Meteorological Hazards

  • Flood
  • Drought
  • Fire (e.g., forest, urban)
  • Snow, ice, hail, avalanche
  • Windstorm, cyclone, hurricane, tornado, dust/sand storm
  • Extreme temperatures
  • Lightning strikes
  • Geomagnetic storm

Biological Hazards

  • Diseases that impact humans or animals:
    • Anthrax
    • West Nile virus
    • Foot and mouth disease
    • Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS)
    • Influenza pandemic
    • Bovine Spongiform Encephalopathy (BSE)
    • Ebola Virus Disease (EVD)
  • Animal or insect infestation or damage
