What is Prevention/Mitigation? (continued)

The Government of Canada’s approach to emergency prevention/mitigation has several facets:

  • a foundation built on legislative acts and policies that guides the government’s actions and expenditures related to prevention/mitigation
  • a systematic approach to mitigating emergencies
  • structural and non-structural measures to prevent and mitigate risks
  • strategies and measures that strengthen Canada’s resiliency and identify actions to prevent and mitigate the hazards and risks
