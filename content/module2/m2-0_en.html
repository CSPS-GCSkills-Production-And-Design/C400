Introduction

Welcome to the module on emergency Prevention/Mitigation.

While prevention and mitigation activities are one of the four components of emergency management, they don’t always make the news the same way response and recovery efforts do. When was the last time you saw headlines like, “Government of Canada saves thousands of lives with prevention/mitigation” or “Federal efforts result in greatly reduced risks for Canadians”?

Nevertheless, the Government of Canada works tirelessly behind the scenes to reduce the long-term impact and risks associated with natural and human-induced emergencies, as do its provincial, territorial, local and international counterparts.

In this module, we will focus on how the federal government approaches emergency prevention and mitigation responsibilities on behalf of all Canadians.
