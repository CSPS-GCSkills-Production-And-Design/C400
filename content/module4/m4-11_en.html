Federal Response Plan: FERP

When a coordinated Government of Canada response is required, the Federal Emergency Response Plan (FERP) is the all-hazards plan that coordinates efforts.

Click on each tab to learn more about the FERP.

Purpose

The FERP is designed to harmonize emergency response efforts by the federal and provincial/territorial governments, non-governmental organizations and the private sector.

Scope

The FERP applies to domestic emergencies and international emergencies with a domestic impact. It does not replace or infringe on any event-specific departmental plan or area of responsibility.

Strategic Objectives
  • save lives
  • protect property and the environment
  • maintain law, order and national security
  • maintain public confidence
  • reduce economic and social losses
