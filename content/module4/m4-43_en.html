Recap – Lesson 5: The People

What do you need to know about the people who respond to emergencies?

Depending on the type and scale of the emergency, all jurisdictions in Canada have their own response mechanisms—people—ready to provide emergency services.

Here is a recap of various examples of emergency responders at the different levels of government.
