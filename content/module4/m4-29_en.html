Federal System: The Federal Emergency Response Management System (FERMS)

Let’s start with the Federal Emergency Response Management System (FERMS).

The Government Operations Centre uses the FERMS to coordinate its activities with those of other government departments.

The FERMS is based on both the Incident Command System and the Treasury Board Secretariat’s Integrated Risk Management Framework.

The FERMS incorporates the following key principles and objectives:

  • flexible and scalable
  • standardized
  • concurrent jurisdictional response
  • constant information sharing
  • ongoing jurisdictional coordination
