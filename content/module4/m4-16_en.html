EM in Action: Emergency Response Plans

In the case of the H1N1 pandemic, all levels of government were prepared to respond to the influenza pandemic thanks to their respective emergency response plans. When the H1N1 event was unfolding, a variety of plans were put into action. Here are a few examples from different jurisdictions.

As with any national emergency, after the danger has passed, these plans are reviewed to incorporate lessons learned to strengthen areas needing improvement.

Did You Know?

The Canadian Pandemic Influenza Plan for the Health Sector was developed in advance of the 2009 H1N1 pandemic.

The Plan was created by federal and provincial/territorial officials, as well as more than 200 experts, to help Canada prepare for and respond to a pandemic influenza outbreak.
