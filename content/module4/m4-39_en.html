Heavy Urban Search and Rescue

Heavy Urban Search and Rescue (HUSAR) teams locate trapped persons in collapsed structures and other entrapments using specially trained dogs and electronic search equipment. The teams breach, shore, lift and remove structural components, use heavy construction equipment to remove debris, and medically treat and transfer victims.

Public Safety Canada, in conjunction with the cities of Vancouver, Calgary, Toronto and Halifax, and the Province of Manitoba, have established national HUSAR capacities that can deploy anywhere across Canada when needed.
