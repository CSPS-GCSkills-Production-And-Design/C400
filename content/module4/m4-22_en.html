Federal Departmental Emergency Operation Centres

Many federal departments have important responsibilities or functions in terms of responding to emergencies. These departments play a central role in response to emergency events and have their own departmental emergency operations centres. These centres work closely with the GOC in support of a federal response to an emergency.

As you review this chart, look to see if your department has an Emergency Operation Centre (EOC).
