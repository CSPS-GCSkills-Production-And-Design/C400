National System: The National Emergency Response System (continued)

The NERS is the critical link between the FERMS and the provincial/territorial systems during an emergency response. The following five key functions are common to all jurisdictions thereby ensuring the coordination of all federal, provincial and territorial activities.

  • Situational Awareness
  • Risk Assessment
  • Planning
  • Logistics
  • Public Communications
