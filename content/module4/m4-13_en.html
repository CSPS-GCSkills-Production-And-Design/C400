Federal Response Plan: Emergency Support Functions

Currently the Government of Canada has identified 13 emergency support functions (ESFs) and has designated a primary department or agency responsible for each of these functions in response to an emergency.

Knowing which department is responsible for which actions during an emergency allows an efficient response and minimizes confusion about roles and responsibilities.
