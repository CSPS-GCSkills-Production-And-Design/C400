Ontario

In Ontario, the USAR (Urban Search and Rescue) and the Urban Search and Rescue CBRNE (Chemical, Biological, Radiological, Nuclear and Explosives) Response Team (called UCRT) are units of highly trained specialists created to deal with disasters and terrorist threats.  These teams were created after the attacks on the United States on September 11, 2001.

Northwest Territories

In the Northwest Territories, the Community Emergency Response Team (C.E.R.T. NWT) operates as a volunteer organization providing operational and logistical support to all federal, territorial, and municipal emergency services, rescue services and community aid services upon request.
