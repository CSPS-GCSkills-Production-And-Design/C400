Local Emergency Response Systems

There are several types of incident management systems used across Canada to manage emergencies at the local level, one being the Incident Command System (ICS).

This system is designed to promote decision-making, communication, cooperation and coordination during response activities to protect people, property and the environment.

Most incident command systems are based on five major functions.

Did You Know?

The success of the ICS rests with its modular organization, use of common terminology, command structure, span of control and resource management.
